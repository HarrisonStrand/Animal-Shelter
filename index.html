Animals Bizzare

Ralph The Kiwi

Having smuggled his way to the United States on a cruise ship gone horribly wrong, Ralph is in need of a gentle home. He enjoys head scratches and fruit.

Winston The Ring-Tailed Lemur

Originally trained as a personal butler, Winston is an expert at answering emails, making coffee in the morning, and bringing you mimosas poolside. Always needs tasks and attention. Only answers to "Winston" in a whining british accent.

Bob The Cat

Struggling with an identity crisis, Bob sometimes thinks that he's a dog. He barks, chases balls, and feels aggressive towards bones. Please sympathize with his struggles as he goes through life.
